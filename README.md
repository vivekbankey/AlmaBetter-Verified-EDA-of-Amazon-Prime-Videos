AlmaBetter Verified EDA of Amazon Prime Videos
Project Overview:-

This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Amazon Prime Video dataset. The objective is to uncover content trends, genre distribution patterns, and country-wise insights to derive meaningful business recommendations.

This project is part of the AlmaBetter Verified Data Analytics Capstone Program.


Business Objective :-

The primary goals of this analysis are:

Understand content distribution across genres

Identify country-wise content production trends

Analyze growth trends over the years

Discover patterns in movies vs TV shows

Generate actionable business insights


Tools & Technologies Used:-

Python

Pandas

NumPy

Matplotlib

Seaborn

Jupyter Notebook


Dataset Description:-

The dataset contains information about Amazon Prime Video content including:

Title

Genre

Director

Cast

Country

Release Year

Rating

Duration

Type (Movie/TV Show)



Key Analysis Performed:-

Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

Handling Missing Values

Genre Distribution Analysis

Country-wise Content Analysis

Year-wise Growth Trend Analysis

Content Type Comparison (Movies vs TV Shows)



Key Insights:-

Majority of content belongs to Drama and Comedy genres.

The United States contributes the highest number of titles.

Significant growth in content addition observed after 2015.

Movies dominate the platform compared to TV Shows.



Business Recommendations:-

Increase investment in high-performing genres such as Drama and Comedy.

Expand content production in emerging markets.

Focus on original TV Shows to balance content mix.

Analyze audience preferences region-wise for targeted content strategy.



Conclusion:-

This analysis demonstrates how data-driven insights can support strategic decision-making in content acquisition and platform growth.
